Homemade Chili

Description

Easy Instant Pot chili made with ground beef, beans, and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 red pepper, chopped
  • Handful of mushrooms, chopped
  • 2-4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups cooked red kidney beans
  • 800ml diced tomatoes
  • 3 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (or more if needed)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Set Instant Pot to Saute. Wait until indicator says HOT.
  2. Season ground beef generously with kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper. Add 1 tbsp olive oil in Instant Pot. Ensure to coat the oil over whole bottom of pot.
  3. Add ground beef in Instant Pot. At the 5-minute mark, remove the ground beef juice and reserve it in a small mixing bowl. Allow the ground beef to brown. Stir occasionally until they are slightly crisped and browned (~5-7 minutes). Taste and adjust the seasoning with more salt and pepper.
  4. Add onion, red pepper, mushrooms, minced garlic, and spices. Saute for about 5 minutes until the spices start to release their fragrance. Stir frequently.
  5. Pour back in ground beef liquid from Step 1. Fully deglaze the bottom of the pot by scrubbing all the flavourful brown bits with a spoon.
  6. Add kidney beans. Mix well. Add diced tomatoes with all the juice on top. Do not mix.
  7. Pressure cook at High Pressure for 10 minutes + Natural Release. Open the lid carefully.
  8. Serve with toppings of your choice and enjoy!
